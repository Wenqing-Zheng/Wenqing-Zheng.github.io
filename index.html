About Me

I'm a currently enrolled Ph.D. student at the VITA group at the University of Texas at Austin under the supervision of Dr. Zhangyang (Atlas) Wang. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in June 2018. From August 2018 to December 2020, I worked as a research assistant with professor Nuria González Prelcic in UT. My current research interests include graph neural networks, decentrolized control, compressive sensing and meta-learning.
